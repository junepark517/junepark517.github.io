Who am I?

I am an associate consultant at Putnam Associates, a strategy consulting firm focused on life sciences. I studied Chemical and Biological engineer at MIT, with a special interest in drug delivery, polymer and life sciences. My undergraduate research experiences include nanoparticle drug delivery and ultrasonic drug delivery platform at Langer Lab, polymer thin films for drug delivery at Trout-Novartis Lab, thermal control device design for scratch indentation platform technology from Materials in Extreme Environments (XLab). Between MIT and research, I like to play the violin and take photographs.
